The App

What is DIVENDO?

DIVENDO helps many different groups of people learn about finance and develop financial literacy. Through built-in lessons and video tutorials, users can learn everything from compound interest to arbitrage strategies.

Users can test their knowledge in the virtual stock simulator and compete with friends and family through quizzes and challenges. The platform makes financial education accessible, engaging, and fun for everyone.
