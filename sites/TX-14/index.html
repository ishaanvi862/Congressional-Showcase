VISIONWARRIORS

Computer vision empowering independence for the visually impaired.

Education Health Accessibility

Seeing the World, Differently

Vision Warriors uses OCR, color detection, and text-to-speech to help visually impaired individuals identify colors and read text from images or live webcam feeds, translating the visual world into clear audio feedback.

The App

Accessibility Through Computer Vision

👁

Color Detection

Identifies object colors from uploaded images or a live webcam feed, making the visual world accessible through clear audio descriptions.

📄

OCR Text Recognition

Reads printed text from any image using optical character recognition, converting visual text into structured data ready for speech output.

🔊

Text-to-Speech

Converts all detected text into natural spoken audio, empowering users to hear what they cannot see with clear, reliable feedback.

🎥

Live Webcam Feed

Real-time processing of live camera input, enabling instant identification and audio output without needing to take or upload a photo.
