The Platform

AI-Powered Research Assistant

Vantage AI leverages Cerebras LLM agents and Hugging Face sentence-transformer models to perform semantic search across research papers. It helps researchers and students quickly find relevant answers grounded in scientific literature.

The platform incorporates Natural Language Inference (NLI) models for source stance analysis, allowing users to understand not just what the research says, but how different sources relate to and support each other.

Semantic Search
Find relevant papers by meaning, not just keywords
LLM Agents
Cerebras-powered agents synthesize answers from sources
Stance Analysis
NLI models reveal how sources agree or conflict
Grounded Answers
Every response is traceable to real scientific papers
