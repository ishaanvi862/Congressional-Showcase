The Platform

About Youth Policy Pulse

Youth Policy Pulse is an interactive platform showing real-time Congressional bills with clear, accessible summaries. Users can like or dislike bills based on their personal viewpoints, making it easy to engage with legislation that affects their lives.

The platform also includes a legislative process overview, helping students understand how bills become laws. By combining real-time data with intuitive interactions, Youth Policy Pulse makes civic participation approachable for the next generation.
