System Overview

Disease-to-Mutation Computational Framework

delta_atlas --describe

Delta Protein Atlas is a computational framework that integrates variant databases, deep learning protein structure prediction, and binding site analysis to connect diseases to specific proteins and mutations. The platform processes 1.2 billion SNPs, maps 223,450 variants across 13,314 genetic diseases, and uses ESM3 7B for structure prediction.

By unifying disease-gene associations with protein-level structural analysis, researchers can trace the functional impact of mutations from clinical phenotype through protein sequence all the way to three-dimensional structure and binding site disruption.
