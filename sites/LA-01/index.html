The Game

Risk-Free Strategy Gaming

AIR Poker is a web-based poker platform featuring AI opponent gameplay, structured betting mechanics, hand evaluation, and showdown systems. Designed for casual players seeking to learn strategy without financial risk.

Built with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS paired with a Firebase backend, AIR Poker delivers smooth gameplay with intelligent AI opponents that challenge players to improve their decision-making skills in a safe environment.
