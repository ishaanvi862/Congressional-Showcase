The Platform

Financial literacy, reimagined

InvestNYou is a comprehensive financial literacy web app that teaches personal finance, investing, and money management through interactive courses, quizzes, and hands-on stock simulation tools. It takes users from the fundamentals all the way to confident financial decision-making.
