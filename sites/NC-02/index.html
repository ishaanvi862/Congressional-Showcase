The App

What is MotionBloom?

MotionBloom is designed for patients with Parkinson's disease and tremor disorders. Using the device camera, it employs computer vision and machine learning to track hand movement in real time and classify tremor severity.

The core innovation is movement-responsive video gating: video playback pauses when tremor increases or movement becomes unstable, creating a natural biofeedback loop that encourages motor stabilization without explicit instruction. Sensitivity thresholds are fully adjustable to accommodate a wide range of patient needs.
