🌼 The App

What is Daisy?

Daisy is an AI assistant that leverages B.R.I. technology to help teams manage tasks and communication with ease. The app remembers context from previous interactions and personalizes its responses to each user, creating a seamless and intuitive experience for group coordination.

Whether you're managing a club, a sports team, or a business group, Daisy keeps communication consistent and organized, reducing the chaos that comes with large group coordination.
