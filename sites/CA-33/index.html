Protecting Communities from Invisible Threats

Lungitude+ monitors carcinogenic particulate matter in real-time, combining EPA and CDC data with AI-powered health guidance to help communities make informed decisions during wildfire events.

Good Moderate Unhealthy Hazardous Severe

The Creators

Bettina Lee & Elena Nguyen

We are high school juniors from Rancho Cucamonga, California. Motivated by experiencing the effects of wildfire smoke firsthand, we founded Project Pulmonary, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to firefighter pulmonary rehabilitation. That experience inspired us to build Lungitude+ to help protect entire communities from the invisible dangers of wildfire particulate matter.

The App

Particulate Monitoring & Education
