Building equitable healthcare access through technology

IL-11 • Aurora, Illinois

About Me

Ishaanvi is a passionate high school junior, 2026 TEDx speaker, and Girls Who Code alumna. She built InsureLit to address the complexity and inequity of healthcare access in America.

Many people struggle to understand what healthcare services they are entitled to. InsureLit simplifies this by making healthcare information accessible, understandable, and actionable.

InsureLit Features

📄 Smart Document Scanner

Scans insurance cards and documents, translating complex medical jargon into simple, clear explanations of coverage.

Uninsured Mode

Switch to a personalized experience with Medicaid info, healthcare rights, and options for those without insurance.

AI Health Assistant

Ask health-related questions and receive answers tailored to your insurance details and personal context.

Live Resource Map

Find nearby clinics and health events with real-time updates based on your location.

Health Calendar

Save, track, and manage appointments or community health events in one place.
