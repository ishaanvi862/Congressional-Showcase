About the Creator

Adi Khurana is a senior at Moorestown High School with deep interests in technology and artificial intelligence for public safety. Beyond coding, Adi has built a business, published research, and teaches coding to members of his community.

Driven by the belief that technology can be a powerful force for keeping people safe, Adi created AlertNow as a direct contribution toward protecting students and staff in schools across the country.

System Overview

About AlertNow

AlertNow is a multi-modal threat detection system combining computer vision (YOLOv8) and audio analysis using Convolutional Neural Networks. The system analyzes live CCTV feeds for weapons and gunshot sounds, sending real-time alerts via SMS, email, and an interactive dashboard.

By fusing both audio and video analysis, AlertNow provides a more reliable detection system than single-modality approaches, enabling faster emergency response and helping protect students and staff in schools.
