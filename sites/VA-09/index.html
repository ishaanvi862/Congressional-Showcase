🏆 Education 🤝 Social 🌟 Gamified

Achievement Unlocked: Community Builder

Project Rush brings discovery, hour tracking, achievements, and leaderboards together in one platform, making volunteering accessible and engaging for high school students.

The Builder

Nathan Chen

I am a junior at Blacksburg High School. I began coding in Swift during elementary school and have since explored multiple programming languages. My passion for building software that serves communities drove me to create Project Rush, a platform that makes volunteering more accessible and engaging for students.

The App

What Project Rush Does

1

Discover Opportunities

Browse and find local volunteer projects tailored to students in your community.

2

Track Your Hours

Log service hours automatically and build a verified service resume over time.

3

Earn Achievements

Unlock badges and milestones as you volunteer, gamifying the community service experience.

4

Climb the Leaderboard

Compete with classmates and see who can make the biggest impact in your community.
