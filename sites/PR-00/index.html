The Creator

Meet Lorena Pachiardi

Lorena is a junior developer from San Juan, Puerto Rico. She began coding in middle school building robots, and has since focused her energy on leveraging technology for meaningful community impact. GoldenCarePR represents her commitment to using software to improve the lives of Puerto Rico's elderly population and their caregivers.

The App

What is GoldenCarePR?

GoldenCarePR is a mobile application designed to support seniors and caregivers across Puerto Rico. It centralizes information on government services, benefits, legal rights, digital safety, and health resources into a single, bilingual interface serving both English and Spanish speakers.

The platform bridges information gaps that leave many elderly Puerto Ricans without access to critical resources. By bringing everything into one place, GoldenCarePR simplifies what has traditionally been a fragmented, confusing process.
