About PRISM

PRISM addresses pancreatic surgery complications (exceeding 40% rates) through a dual-component system. The pre-operative software handles tumor segmentation and surgical planning, while the mixed-reality headset software detects 10+ critical organs and blood vessels in real-time during surgery.

By combining advanced image segmentation with augmented reality guidance, PRISM gives surgeons unprecedented visibility during complex procedures. Surgeons estimate a potential 10-30% reduction in complication rates when using the system.
