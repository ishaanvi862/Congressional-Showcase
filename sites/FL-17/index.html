The Platform

How It Works

Cognitive Care Assistant is a comprehensive platform combining cognitive assessments, behavioral tracking, and AI-powered analysis to support dementia care. The system integrates hardware sensors including EMG and AMG8833 thermal sensors for movement and sleep monitoring, providing caregivers with actionable insights.

The platform has been verified by healthcare professionals and has secured three organizations for beta testing. It represents a holistic approach to dementia care that bridges software intelligence with real-world sensor data.
