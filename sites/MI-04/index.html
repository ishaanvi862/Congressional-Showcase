The App

Your personal study sanctuary

Study Oasis is a study resource designed for middle and high school students. It offers focus timers to help students stay on task, assignment tracking via organized task lists, and an AI tutoring system that answers questions and explains concepts in a safe, supportive environment.

The app brings together the essential tools students need to study effectively -- all in one place -- creating a calm, focused space for learning.
