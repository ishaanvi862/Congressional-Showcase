The App

What is Swell?

Swell provides senior citizens with brain-engaging games designed for clarity and comfort. Every element — from large, readable text to simple navigation — is built so that older adults feel confident and welcome from the first tap.

The goal is straightforward: help seniors maintain cognitive sharpness and have a genuinely good time doing it. No clutter, no pressure — just the satisfaction of solving a great puzzle.
