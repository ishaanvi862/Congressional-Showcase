The App

What It Does

ElderLink AR is a mobile application featuring two specialized chatbots that deliver customized wellness guidance to elderly users. The app leverages AR camera-tracking technology to provide real-time, interactive health feedback across both Android and iOS.

⚙

How It Works

Under the Hood

Built cross-platform with Kotlin and Swift, ElderLink AR integrates Google ML Kit and Apple ML Kit for machine learning, the Gemini API for conversational AI, and Firebase for reliable backend services.
